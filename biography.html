Biography | 個人簡介

Cindy Shih-Yu Hsieh is a Taiwanese cellist with a deep commitment to performance and musical collaboration. She earned her Master of Music degree from the Mannes School of Music in 2024, where she studied under Jay Campbell, and received her Bachelor of Arts in Music from Soochow University.

An active solo, chamber, and orchestral musician, Cindy has served as principal cellist of the Mannes Orchestra and has participated in major performances at Alice Tully Hall. Her chamber music experience also brought her to Carnegie Hall, where she performed in a featured concert during her graduate studies.

She has appeared at music festivals including Festival Napa Valley’s Blackburn Music Academy, PRISMA, and the Green Lake Chamber Music Festival, and has been awarded top prizes in international chamber music competitions. She has also participated in masterclasses with cellists such as Henry Shapard, Inbal Segev, and Joel Noyes.

In addition to performing, Cindy is also an experienced cello and piano teacher.


謝仕妤是一位來自台灣的大提琴家，專注於獨奏、室內樂與樂團演出，並積極參與多元的音樂合作計畫。她於2024年取得紐約曼尼斯音樂學院碩士學位，師承傑出大提琴家 Jay Campbell，並於東吳大學獲得音樂學士學位。

身為活躍的演奏者，Cindy 曾擔任曼尼斯管絃樂團首席，並參與多場於林肯中心 Alice Tully 音樂廳舉辦的重要音樂會。她也曾於卡內基音樂廳演出室內樂作品，展現其豐富的舞台經驗。

她曾參與多個國際音樂節，包括加州的 Napa Valley 黑本音樂學院、加拿大 PRISMA 音樂節，以及美國 Green Lake 室內樂音樂節，並在多項國際室內樂比賽中屢獲佳績。她亦曾參加多位知名大提琴家的大師班，包括 Henry Shapard、Inbal Segev 與 Joel Noyes 等。

除了演出外，Cindy 亦積極從事教學工作，教授大提琴與鋼琴。
